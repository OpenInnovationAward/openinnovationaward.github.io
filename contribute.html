Judging

If you contribute $50 or more, you are invited to cast one vote to help decide the winners! Voting will follow a multiple-winner Instant Runoff Voting scheme. Please see the applicant information page for details on the selection criteria.

At this time, we have not been recognized as a nonprofit by the IRS and contributions are not tax-deductible. This is only likely to affect you if you itemize deductions.

The Future

In future years, we will take the previous years' portfolio of submissions and solicit matching and in-kind contributions from local businesses and Maker/DIY/open source organizations. We are also working on ideas for a minigrant process to provide project seed funds, a mentorship component, and additional prizes for the best submissions in specific categories (music, math, earth science, &etc).
